Block Party

May 4 2019, 11AM-9PM

RSVP

Celebrate music in our community

The Music Village Block party is a celebration of music and culture in our community. Presented by The Music Village, this first-annual, all-day event is free and open to all, 11a-9p on Saturday, May 4, on the 300 block of South Michigan Street (between Wayne and Western).

Something for everyone

The day will feature regional and local artists, performing on the Harman International Mobile Stage. Food, vendors, children's activities and programs, dancers, and community partners will enliven the day. South Bend Brew Werks will offer beer for sale (over 21 with valid photo ID required for wristband).

Sponsors

TMV is committed to paying our artists honoraria their talent. Sponsorships are imperative to making this a viable undertaking, and one which, we anticipate, will become an annual tradition.

The Music Village leadership seeks to make this Block Party a viable and first-annual event. To that end, TMV seeks sponsors to help underwrite costs for artists, infrastructure, promotion, and more. If you or someone/organization/corporation you know might be interested in prominently supporting the event, please contact tmv@themusicvillage.org
